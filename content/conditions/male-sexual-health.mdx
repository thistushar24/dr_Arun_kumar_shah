Male sexual health issues are common and treatable. Our clinic provides a private, judgment-free environment to discuss and treat these sensitive conditions.

## Conditions We Treat
- Erectile Dysfunction (ED)
- Premature Ejaculation
- Male Infertility
- Low Testosterone (Hypogonadism)
- Peyronie's Disease

## Our Approach
Dr. Arun Shah conducts a comprehensive evaluation including hormonal assessments, vascular studies, and semen analysis to identify the underlying cause and recommend the most effective treatment.
