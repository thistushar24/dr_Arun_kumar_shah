# Kidney Stones

Kidney stones are hard deposits made of minerals and salts that form inside your kidneys.

## Symptoms
- Severe pain in the side and back
- Pain that radiates to the lower abdomen
- Pain that comes in waves
- Pain on urination
- Pink, red or brown urine
- Cloudy or foul-smelling urine

## Advanced Laser Treatments
We specialize in minimally invasive treatments such as:
- **RIRS (Retrograde Intrarenal Surgery)**
- **PCNL (Percutaneous Nephrolithotomy)**
- **URSL (Ureteroscopic Lithotripsy)**

These modern techniques allow for faster recovery, minimal pain, and no large incisions compared to traditional open surgery.
